Ecological Education and Public Engagement

2010‚Äì2020

  • Over the past decade, I have carried out numerous environmental education seminars and workshops designed to raise awareness about biodiversity and ecosystem functioning. These activities have addressed the importance of birds and mammals in providing ecosystem services, the pressures they face from human activities, and the value of species identification to promote knowledge and conservation. Beyond classroom seminars, I have organized field-based workshops focused on wildlife tracking, bird and mammal identification, and guided nature interpretation routes by bicycle. The programs have also included nest box and feeder building, citizen science experiments exploring water pollution and geology, and outreach activities such as environmental film forums. All of these initiatives aim to connect participants with nature through science, and to strengthen their ecological understanding and environmental responsibility.
