Outreach

Alongside my research, I genuinely enjoy sharing ecology with a broad audience through media work, public talks, and educational activities, aiming to bring our scientific results closer to those who fund it, to the people making decisions on the land, and—above all—to stay connected with the real world beyond the lab.
